About Us

Welcome to BitFundMe, a Blockchain decentralized platform and premier destination for passionate individuals, entrepreneurs, start-ups, charity organizations and businesses looking to bring their ideas to life using cryptocurrency.

Our mission as a crowdfunding platform is to:

  • Promote transparency,
  • Increase trust,
  • Minimize transaction fees,
  • Promote inclusivity,
  • Create decentralization and
  • Enhance Integrity.

We believe in creating a level playing ground where anyone can share their vision and gain support from a global community by eliminating intermediaries through the use of Smart Contracts.

BitFundMe is poised to solve the major challenges inherent in other crowdfunding platforms such as:

  • Restriction access to selected regions/countries of the world.
  • Reduce high transaction charges
  • Mitigate risk
  • Technological drawbacks in developing countries
  • Encourages acceptance/spread of cryptocurrency in developing countries.

About Our Company

We started as a learning group formed by graduates of the Blockchain scholarship programme organized by the National Information Technology Development Agency (NITDA).

As a quest to put into practice knowledge acquired during the programme, this project was conceived and as the saying goes, the rest is history.

Team Members

BitFundMe as a company comprises of talented individuals from virtually every walk of life.
Full stack Developers, Frontend and Backend Developers, UX UI Designers, graphics artists, Cyber Security Expert Engineers, Entrepreneurs, lawyers etc make up this novel organization. The idea of a company is fall out of a unanimous decision of a few number of the original learning group.

BitFundMe is different from other platforms because it has integrated the power of Blockchain Technology and Crowdfunding, thereby setting is apart from its competitions.

Blockchain enables transparency, trust and integrity that is why we have specifically chosen to build our platform on it.

Join our platform today and become part of our novel, vibrant and innovative community.

Here you will experience the joy of bringing your ideas to life, discover extraordinary projects and be an active participant in shaping a brighter future. Bitfundme is a platform where dreams become reality and passion funds it's purpose.
